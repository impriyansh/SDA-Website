About Us.

Founded in the year 2016, SDA INDUSTRIES is trusted and established Manufacturing firm, providing premium quality of Industrial Steel Wires, and Wire Nails . Our offered product is manufactured on quality specifications and generating us maximum benefit for its ease of installation, industrial design, reliable performance, and practicable cost.
Our organization is backed by the state-of-the-art infrastructural facility for manufacturing our offered product range.Our manufacturing unit is installed with all the modern machines, tools and equipment to increase the production rate. Additionally, our production unit also helps us in meeting the bulk requirements of our valuable customers within the stipulated time schedule. Moreover, we are backed by a skilled team of hard-working professionals, who are highly responsible in the success of our company.
Our qualified staff will help with the selection of goods and give all necessary advice and guidance on the use of products. We can offer our clients a wide range of binding wire and wire nails at the best prices.
In our factory there are three pillars of work:
• High quality of all goods and services
• Reasonable pricing
• Careful attention to every clients.
Strict quality control, product quality to meet international standards, we have the full implementation of ISO9001 quality management system.
